Biography

Shaoxun Liu is currently a Research Fellow with the College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University. His research interests include heavy-duty wheel-legged robots, vehicle dynamics and control, fault-tolerant control, force estimation, and autonomous unmanned systems.

Research Interests

  • Heavy-duty wheel-legged robotic systems
  • Vehicle dynamics and motion control
  • Fault diagnosis and fault-tolerant control
  • State, force, and disturbance estimation
  • Autonomous unmanned systems

Selected Publications
